MasterClock Light is a simplified chess clock for chess, wargames, and tabletop games.

This is the Light build: every classic timing mode, ready-made presets, and arbitre mode (manual time adjustment for a tournament director), with no extra settings screens to dig through. If you want behavior/display/audio customization or the full toolset (Notebook, Scoreboard, etc.), look for MasterClock (Complete) or MasterClock Standard instead.

Classic time controls:
* Sudden Death, Fischer, Bronstein, US Delay
* Byoyomi (Japanese, Canadian, Progressive)
* Hourglass, Gong, Hidden/Random time
* Move counters, fast-move variants, time banking

Also included:
* A large library of ready-made presets (blitz, rapid, classical, and named tournament time controls)
* Arbitre mode for manual time correction during a game

MasterClock collects no data, contains no ads, no trackers, and needs no network connection to work.
