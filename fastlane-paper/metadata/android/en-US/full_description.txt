MasterClock Paper is a chess clock built specifically for E-Ink devices (such as Mudita Pure/Kompakt-style hardware), using a pure black-and-white interface designed for E-Ink screens: high contrast, no gradients, minimal redraws.

It covers three timing modes: Timer (Sudden Death), Fischer, and Move Timer, plus a "Reverse colors" toggle, keep-screen-awake, sound, and haptic feedback options tuned for E-Ink hardware.

MasterClock collects no data, contains no ads, no trackers, and needs no network connection to work.
