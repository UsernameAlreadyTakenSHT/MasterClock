MasterClock Mini is the bare-essentials build of MasterClock, a chess clock for chess, wargames, and tabletop games.

This build sticks to three timing modes: Sudden Death, Bonus (Fischer increment), and Move Timer (Standard). No extra settings screens, no presets library, no arbitre mode — just a clock you can set up in a few taps. If you want more timing modes or extra tools, look for MasterClock Light, Standard, or Complete instead.

MasterClock collects no data, contains no ads, no trackers, and needs no network connection to work.
