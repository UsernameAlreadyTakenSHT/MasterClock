MasterClock is a chess clock for chess, wargames, and tabletop games. It handles classic time controls as well as more complex multi-phase formats.

This is the Complete build, which includes every feature. Three other builds exist (Standard, Light, ExtraLight) with progressively fewer options, for players who just want a simpler clock.

Classic time controls:
* Sudden Death, Fischer, Bronstein, US Delay
* Byoyomi (Japanese, Canadian, Progressive)
* Hourglass, Gong, Hidden/Random time
* Move counters, fast-move variants, time banking

Omni mode:
* Sequential game lists with custom round behavior
* Multi-phase turns (Think, Move, Resolve)
* Configurable auto-cutoff per level (Phase, Turn, Round, Game, Session)

Also included:
* Notebook (text, drawing, audio, photo, video, and board notes)
* Scoreboard and game history with PGN/PDN/KIF export
* Mini-games: coin toss, dice roll, short straw, blindfold trainer, and more
* QR code sharing and Bluetooth chess board support
* Minimal black-and-white interface

MasterClock collects no data, contains no ads, no trackers, and needs no network connection to work.
