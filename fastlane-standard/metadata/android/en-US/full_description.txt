MasterClock Standard is a chess clock for chess, wargames, and tabletop games. It handles classic time controls as well as more complex multi-phase formats.

This is the Standard build: every timing mode plus behavior, display, and audio customization, presets, and arbitre mode. It leaves out the Complete build's extra tools (Notebook, Scoreboard, mini-games, QR sharing, Bluetooth board support). If you want those too, look for MasterClock (Complete) instead.

Classic time controls:
* Sudden Death, Fischer, Bronstein, US Delay
* Byoyomi (Japanese, Canadian, Progressive)
* Hourglass, Gong, Hidden/Random time
* Move counters, fast-move variants, time banking

Customization:
* Flag behavior (Freeze, Flag, Negative, Reverse)
* Display options: time format, decimals, period indicators
* Sound and haptic feedback for player switches and countdowns
* Presets and arbitre mode for tournament use

MasterClock collects no data, contains no ads, no trackers, and needs no network connection to work.
